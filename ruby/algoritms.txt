#Task: Using an elevator

-Stand in front of the dial pad with the up and down buttons next to the elevator door.
-Push the upper button (maybe labeled "up" or with an upward pointing arrow head)
-Wait for the elevator to flash an upward pointing arrow or an "up" sign
  -If the light flash comes from the down arrow or sign, don't get in
-Once the doors open let people out first and get in
-Press the button of the floor you want to go to:
  -If you re going out, press "lobby", "L" or "1"
  -If you want to go to your appartment, press 6 regardless of the initital floor you where at
-If the door takes more than 15 seconds to close push the "close" button
-As long as the elevator stops and opens the door while flashing signs that are not the same as the floor or lobby you want to go, remain on the elevator
-If/When the elevator flashes the light corresponding to the floor you dialed, wait for the doors to open and exit

#Task: Making tea in the morning

-Go to the kitchen area via the shortest way possible
-Get the electric kettle on the counter to your left and take it to the sink
-Open the kettle's top place under faucet
-Open the tap to fill the kettle with water
-When the water reaches the 3 cup mark on the water level window, close the tap
-Close the kettle top and replace on the electric base on the counter
-Check if base is connected to an outlet
  -If it is connected, skip to next step
  -If it's not connected, plug into outlet
-Press the only buttton on the kettle's side
-While the water is boiling
  -Get a cup from the cupboard in front of you, place on counter
  -Get a tea bag from tea box in front of you on the counter
  -Open tea bag and place content inside the cup with the string hanging on the outside
-Wait until the button makes a popping noise and returns to its original position
-Grab the kettle by the handle and place beak hovering inside he cup
-Tilt the kettle just enough so the water starts poring into the cup
  -If water starts spilling down, re-align kettle to top and center of the cup's mouth
-When the water reaches 4/5's of the cup's body stop pouring
-Replace kettle on electric base
-Wait three minutes for tea to steep
-Now you can drink your tea
